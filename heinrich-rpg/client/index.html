📖 How to Play

Basic Controls

  • Choice Buttons (A, B, C, D): Click to select a presented option
  • Free Input: Type any action and press Enter
  • Quick Actions: Common actions at the bottom of the screen

Tips

  • Be specific in your actions — "Search the dusty bookshelf" is better than "Look around"
  • Talk to NPCs to learn about the world and find opportunities
  • Manage your hunger, fatigue, and health — neglect has consequences
  • Skills improve through use — practice makes perfect
  • Save often! The game auto-saves every turn, but exports are good backups

The World Lives

The world continues whether you act or not. NPCs pursue their own goals, seasons change, and consequences unfold. Time is precious — use it wisely.
